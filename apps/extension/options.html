Supported LLMs

  • ChatGPT (chat.openai.com)
  • Claude Web (claude.ai)
  • Google Gemini (gemini.google.com)
  • Microsoft Copilot (copilot.microsoft.com)
  • HuggingFace Spaces

How It Works

  1. Visit any supported LLM website
  2. Click the "💾 Save to Unimatrix" button that appears
  3. Paste your conversation or select "Auto-Capture"
  4. Add context tags (optional) and importance level
  5. Click "Save Memory" — encrypted automatically

Privacy

• All memories encrypted end-to-end with AES-256-GCM
• Your encryption password stays on your device
• Server stores only ciphertext
• You control your data completely
