# Risk Assessment Procedure (SOC2 / HIPAA)

Purpose:
- Identify, evaluate, and treat risks to systems and PHI.

Frequency:
- Full formal assessment annually; targeted assessments after major changes.

Steps:
1. Scope & Asset Inventory: list systems, data flows, PHI stores.
2. Threats & Vulnerabilities: enumerate likely threats and known vulnerabilities.
3. Likelihood & Impact Scoring: use consistent scale (e.g., 1-5).
4. Risk Rating: calculate risk = likelihood × impact; prioritize top risks.
5. Treatment Plan: accept/mitigate/transfer/avoid with owners, timelines, and controls.
6. Residual Risk: document and accept by business owner.
7. Tracking & Review: track mitigations in a registry; review monthly until closed.

Deliverables:
- Risk register, remediation roadmap, executive summary.

Evidence:
- Assessment artifacts, meeting notes, and sign-offs preserved for audits.
